Frequently Asked Questions

No support? What if I have a billing problem, or if there are infrastructure problems?

Fear not, we will always support our platform. If you suspect there is a problem on our end you are welcome to email us with the relevant information.

Why don't you offer support?

It is expensive to provide good support to customers. To provide support, most providers bake extra cost into their plan prices making everybody pay more for their services. Not everybody needs support though, so if you are a capable systems administrator you pay to subsidize the support for people who have no idea how to configure their system, need help with Apache or other system administration realated questions. We decided that isn't right, if you don't need support you shouldn't have to pay for it so we got rid of the support team and made our prices 20% cheaper than bigger competitors.

What if I need support for administrative tasks?

Honestly your best resource is Google. If you want to know how to setup Apache search "How do I set up Apache?" in Google. If you need more help than that you might want to consult sites like ServerFault or a forum related to the problem you are having.

What technology do you use?

Our hypervisors use QEMU/KVM for virtualization and our panel is provided by Virtkick.

Can you guys add {feature}?

We'll do our best! Email us some details of what you need and we'll see if we can make it happen, no promises though!
